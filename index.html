Female and More

FAM

Female and More

  • - Product Management
  • - Product Design

Overview

This project is about how to design and develop a platform for girls from the ages of 13years and above, who may have issues with self-esteem. The platform gives the girls an opportunity to get involved in planned activities and stories about ladies who have made a mark in various sectors so as to build their confidence and self-worth.

Problem

The client actually started building a community of girls with self-esteem and self-confidence issues. They succeeded in getting about 3,000 girls into a Telegram chat group and stated running a 30days self-esteem program. It was quite tedious, manual and difficult to keep up.

Solution

Female And More (FAM) is an online community designed to increase the Self-esteem, voice and leadership skills of young women in Africa so they can think big, pursue greatness and reach their fullest potentials in any endeavor of their choice

Users

The platform is for young African women between the ages of 13 - 35years who may need to increase their self-esteem and build their self-confidence.

Key Features

The FAM project uses a 30 days’ Self-esteem and leadership module comprising easy-to-read summary stories of inspiring Africa who overcame social barriers and achieved their dreams and greatness in their chosen fields of endeavors.

Abuja Wifi

AbujaWifi

Access Solutions

  • - Product Management
  • - Product Design

Overview

This project is born out of a need to create a faster and more reliable internet service within the metropolitan part of Abuja. The project is focus on showing

Problem

Internet services within the Abuja Metrololis has been quite poor. Users have quite a number of options, many of which are will require a high cost in installation especially for broadband internet.

Solution

AbujaWifi is a broadband service with little or no cost in installation. It is both location and web-based. Once the Wifi service is enabled in an area, users can easily connect their devices and just subscribe easily for speed up to 20mbps.

Users

The product targets all categories of internet users within a district or zone in which the service is enabled or available.

Key Features

The AbujaWifi project presents unique, cost effective data plans for customers within the metropolis.
